The Heart of Kaelen

The Kaelen Republic is a nation defined by its soaring, snow-capped peaks and deep-rooted democratic values. Formed from a union of two foundational regions, its system is based on clarity and balance. The name "Kaelen" embodies the unyielding **Stability** of its mountains, providing the bedrock for the nation's high **Aspiration** and enduring **Unity**.
